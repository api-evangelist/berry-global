# Berry Global (berry-global)
Berry Global is a leading global manufacturer and marketer of plastic packaging products, nonwoven specialty materials, and engineered materials for consumer and industrial end markets. In 2024, Berry Global merged with Amcor to form one of the world's largest packaging companies. Berry's products include rigid open top packaging, closures and dispensing systems, flexible packaging, tapes, and engineered materials.
